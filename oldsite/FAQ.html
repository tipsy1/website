Where can I find my FedoraCoin wallet.dat file?

You can find your FedoraCoin wallet.dat file:
WIN: C:\Users\[username]\AppData\Roaming\FedoraCoin\wallet.dat
Mac OSX: ~/Library/Application Support/FedoraCoin/wallet.dat
Linux: /.fedoracoin/wallet.dat
